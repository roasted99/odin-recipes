Mushroom Rice Balls



What is mushroom rice balls?

Vegetarian version of Shan-style Fish Rice (Ngar-hta-min-Nel). A very simple meal with 4 ingredients; oyster mushroom, tomato, garlic and rice. For rice, it is best to use Jasmine rice or Thai home mali rice or Sushi rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Rice, cooked and set to warm
  • 3 (L) Tomato
  • 2 pkt Oyster Mushroom (~ 300 g)
  • 1 head Garlic
  • 1/2 cup Oil
  • A pinch of Turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp Sugar

Steps

  1. Lightly score tomato and place in a sauce pot. Add enough water to cook until skin starts to peel about 5-7 mins. Remove the skin and blend in a blender till watery.
  2. Shred oyster mushroom, wash thorough. Place in a pot, add 1 tsp of salt and cook on medium-low heat till it released juices. Then, off the fire when it is almost dried.
  3. Finely chop or pound the garlic with a mortar and pestle.
  4. Heat up the oil in a frying pan over medium heat. Add garlic and turmeric then, fry till garlic starts to change color or slightly crispy.
  5. Transfer the garlic into a heat proof bowl and let the garlic continue cook with the remaining heat till it is completely crispy. Remain about 1 tsp of oil in the pan.
  6. Pour in the tomato into the same frying pan, add 1 tsp of salt, 1/2 tsp of sugar and cook till it gets thicker.
  7. Pour in the tomato into the same frying pan, add 1 tsp of salt, 1/2 tsp of sugar and cook till it gets thicker.
  8. Place the cooked rice in a big bowl and add 1 tsp of salt. Knead till the rice grains are broken and getting sticky.
  9. Pour in half amount of tomato purée and oyster mushroom. Knead till well combine.
  10. Add more tomato purée and salt to adjust the taste. Mix well then shape into 3-4 inch diameter balls or disc and coat with garlic oil.
  11. To serve, top with crispy garlic and chili oil. Best to go with hot green tea.
