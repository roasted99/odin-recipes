Warm Chickpea Tofu Noodle



What is Warm Chickpea Tofu Noodle

Warm Tofu (Tofu Nway) is one of the popular breakfast which can be found mostly in Shan State. Shan is a generic term for Tai people who live inside Myanmar (Burma).

This tofu is also know as Shan Tofu (Burmese Tofu) and it can be prepared in variety including deep-fried and salad. The main ingredient making this tofu is chickpea. Despite it's name "tofu", it is not made from soybean. So, it is soy-free, vegan and gluten-free.

Ingredients

  • Dried Shan Noodles 1 pkt (Rice noodles)
  • 200 g Besan Flour
  • A pinch of Turmeric Powder
  • 2 Ltr Water
  • Crispy Garlic in Oil
  • Fragrant Sesame Oil
  • Sweet Sauce
  • 1/2 cup Roasted Peanuts, discharge the skin
  • Coriander
  • Light Soy Sauce
  • Chili Oil

Steps

  1. In a big bowl mix all the dried ingredients for tofu; besan flour, salt and Turmeric powder.
  2. Pour in 500 ml of water (divide into 2-3 parts) and stir until it becomes a batter.
  3. Let it sit at least 2 hrs to maximum 8 hrs.
  4. Soak dried noodles with water as per package instruction.
  5. Preparing all the toppings. Heat oil in the sauce pan and occasionally stir minced garlic until light brown. Transfer it into a heat prove bowl and let the garlic continue cook in the hot oil till crispy.
  6. With the same sauce pan, heat up the oil and then let Black Cardamon and Star Anise infuse for 10 mins. Scoop in a spoon of sesame seeds and fry till light brown.
  7. Stir palm sugar and water over medium flame till everything is well dissolved.
  8. Pound/crush roasted peanuts. Chop some coriander.
  9. Once all the toppings are ready, boil 1.5 ltr of water in a soup pot or big frying pan. Stir the besan mixture, then pour into the boiling water slowly with one hand and keep stir with spatula on the other hand.
  10. After 8-10 mins, the mixture will get thicken and fragrant.
  11. Turn the flame to lowest to prevent it become solid.
  12. To serve warm tofu, cook a portion of noodles in boiling water till soft.
  13. Transfer it into the warm tofu pot and coat the noodles with tofu, then scoop into the bowl together with warm tofu.
  14. Add each tbsp of all the prepare toppings on top of warm tofu. Serve immediately. It is best to go with You Tiao.
